What Makes ASA Lovin'?

The Carnegie Mellon University's Asian Students Association, also known as ASA, is the largest student organization on campus. Comprised of students interested in exploring Asian culture and participating in different events throughout the school year, our organization is a fun way to get involved and meet new people. We welcome anyone who is interested in being a part of our family, so feel free to come out!

Throughout the year we are looking for PR members who will attend meetings and get involved in fundraisers and events. To join, just come to our PR meetings every Wednesday at 5:30 in the West Wing TV Lounge and sign up to be on our PR list. We will send you updates about our events so you can meet other ASA members at our social activities! Come early so we can pair you up with a mentor who will tell you more about ASA!

Second semester we build a booth for the Spring Carnival, and it's one of our biggest activities of the year. All the PRs, board members, and even alumni will come out and help build an amazing booth, which will hopefully win first place! Through the times we spend together buidling our booth, ASA members develop close friendships that last a lifetime.

Join Us! Come to PR Meetings and be a PR member. We'll add you to our d-list, just leave us your email here!
